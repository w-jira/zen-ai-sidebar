Auto-Summary Privacy

Auto-summary sends page content to your AI provider. It runs ONLY on allowlisted domains below. A builtin denylist always blocks sensitive sites (banks, email, healthcare, auth pages, etc.) even if accidentally allowlisted.

Exact domains or parent domains. E.g. wikipedia.org matches en.wikipedia.org. Leave blank to disable auto-summary everywhere.
